Spatial Indexing: Hilbert (S2) vs Z-Order (Geohash)

This interactive visualization demonstrates why Google Spanner (S2) can use efficient 1D range scans for spatial queries, while Geohash suffers from fragmentation.

Drag the green query box below. Notice how the Hilbert curve (S2) tends to keep cells within a single continuous numeric range (fewer BETWEEN clauses needed), while the Z-Order curve (Geohash) jumps around, fracturing the query into many disconnected segments.
